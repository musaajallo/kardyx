00 / Synthesis

What we kept from each parent direction.

Why hybrid Brand must read as globally credible (Atlas) but feel safe and warm around a kitchen table (Quiet Game). Character art Lives in product, not in the brand chrome.
System layerDecision
Layout grammar AAtlas's editorial grid · numbered sections · monospaced metadata · clear hierarchy. The skeleton.
Color discipline CQuiet Game's restraint — paper, mist, sumi as ground. AAtlas's clay reserved as the high-energy accent for Power-Up moments only. Ochre as the soft brand divider.
Display typography ANewsreader 600 — editorial serif, warmer than a strict modern, holds weight without shouting.
Body typography A+CInter for product UI, Newsreader 400 italic for editorial body and lore. Newsreader leads when the moment is a moment; Inter leads when the user is doing.
Metadata typography AJetBrains Mono — for codes, party IDs, system text. The federation signal.
Motion A+CSettle-stamp. Faster than C's pure settle, slower than A's punctuated stamp. No bouncy springs. Curve cubic-bezier(0.22, 0.6, 0.2, 1).
Character art BHand-illustrated, but rendered in this restrained palette (sumi line work, single ochre or clay accent per character). Character lives on cards and in flavor moments — not in the logo, not in the chrome.
Voice Editorial-warm. "A round of Kardyx is a kind of attention" — but also "your turn, Musa." Federation tone with a friend's warmth.
