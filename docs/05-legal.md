# 5. Legal & IP Protection Strategy

> **Disclaimer:** This document outlines a strategy. Engage qualified legal counsel in each target jurisdiction before filing.

## 1. Trademark

- Register **KARDYX** as a wordmark in:
  - The Gambia (home jurisdiction)
  - ECOWAS regional protection where available
  - USA (USPTO)
  - EU (EUIPO)
  - UK (IPO)
- Cover relevant Nice Classes:
  - **Class 9** — software, mobile apps
  - **Class 28** — games, playing cards
  - **Class 41** — entertainment services, online gaming

## 2. Copyright

The following are copyrighted as proprietary IP:

- **Concept Note** (this docs folder)
- **Game Logic Architecture** (Power-Up engine, scoring, governance)
- **Visual Designs** (UI, card art, character illustrations)
- **Audio Assets** (Lyric Weaver loops, sound effects)

Copyright is automatic on creation but should be **registered** in jurisdictions where registration grants enforcement advantages (e.g., USA).

## 3. Character IP

Each unique character is a distinct, protectable asset:

- **Trademark** the character name and likeness
- **Copyright** the visual design
- Maintain **character bibles** documenting traits, lore, and design rules
- Reserve rights for **merchandising and licensing**

## 4. Patent (Utility) — Exploratory

Investigate filing a utility patent for:

> **"Dynamic QR/CV Card-to-Mobile Synchronization Logic"** — a system that recognizes physical cards via on-device computer vision and triggers networked, deterministic, replayable game-state mutations across distributed clients.

### Patent Considerations

- Run a **prior art search** before filing
- Consider a **provisional application** first (12-month placeholder)
- File in jurisdictions with strong software-patent enforcement (USA, EU)
- Patent costs are significant — weigh against trade-secret protection

## 5. Defensive Measures

- Maintain a **public dev log** with timestamps for invention dates
- Keep all design docs under version control with signed commits
- NDAs for all contractors, contributors, and beta testers
- Open-source any non-core utilities (CV helpers) under permissive licenses to avoid patent troll friction; keep the **Power-Up engine and game logic** closed-source

## 6. Domain & Brand Assets

- Register `kardyx.com`, `kardyx.io`, `kardyx.app`, `kardyx.gm`
- Reserve handles on major social platforms (Instagram, X, TikTok, YouTube, Discord)
- Register Apple App Store and Google Play developer accounts under the legal entity
